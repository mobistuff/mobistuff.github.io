Old Town of Xanthi

ANDROID

The Old Town of Xanthi is a small place that reveals its secrets to routes that connect the present with the past, history with the culture of everyday life, faith in God with the truth of nature.

  • Organize your walk in Old Town of Xanthi following routes of cultural, religious and architectural interest.
  • Choose to taste local delicacies you can find from a list of 34 restaurants, local sweet shops and taverns.
  • Learn the history of the Old town and share your experiences, pictures and thoughts with your friends and family.
  • Forget using a paper map or a city guide book, with this app you will save a lot of time and it will be much more fun!
